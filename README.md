## Project Overview

GoExplore is a growing European camping and hiking retailer planning an expansion into four new European markets. After the company's data scientist unexpectedly left, CEO Sabah provided a single spreadsheet containing business data and requested a complete analytics solution within two weeks.

The objective of this project was to transform raw business data into actionable insights that could support GoExplore's expansion strategy and improve retailer performance.

The analysis focused on two key business questions:

1. **Market Expansion Analysis**
   - Which four European markets offer the strongest growth opportunities?
   - What market sizes can GoExplore expect in these countries?

2. **Retailer Performance Analysis**
   - Do specialty retailers (e.g., Golf Shops) perform differently from general retailers (e.g., Sports Stores)?
   - Which retailer types generate the highest sales performance?

---

# Business Objectives

### 1. Identify Expansion Opportunities

GoExplore wants to expand into new European markets. This project evaluates potential countries based on:

- Market size
- Customer demand
- Sales potential
- Existing retailer performance
- Product category performance

The goal is to provide data-driven recommendations on where GoExplore should prioritize expansion efforts.

---

### 2. Understand Retailer Performance

The company sells products through different retailer types, including:

- Sports Stores
- Outdoor Shops
- Golf Shops
- Other specialty retailers

This analysis investigates whether retailer specialization influences sales performance.

Key questions:

- Do specialty retailers generate higher revenue?
- Which retailer categories should GoExplore prioritize?
- Are there differences in customer purchasing behavior?
